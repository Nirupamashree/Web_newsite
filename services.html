Services

Discover the comprehensive range of services offered by our invigilator management system:

  • Invigilator Scheduling: Efficiently schedule invigilators for exams using automated features and conflict resolution.
  • Invigilator Training: Access training programs covering exam protocols, security measures, and exceptional situation handling.
  • Exam Room Setup: Simplify the setup process by managing seating arrangements, equipment setup, and exam material distribution.
  • Attendance Tracking: Track invigilator attendance during exams and generate accurate reports for administrative purposes.
  • Communication and Notifications: Streamline communication with invigilators and stakeholders through messaging and notifications.
  • Reporting and Analytics: Gain valuable insights through reports on invigilator performance, workload distribution, and attendance statistics.
  • Security and Integrity: Ensure exam security with invigilator authentication, secure access controls, and monitoring capabilities.
  • Integration and Customization: Seamlessly integrate the system with other platforms and customize it to suit specific requirements.
  • Support and Training: Receive technical support, assistance, and training to maximize the benefits of the system.
