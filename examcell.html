Office of Deputy Controller of Examinations (Dy. CoE)

The Office of Deputy Controller of Examinations is responsible for the conduct of Examinations (Periodicals, End Semester and Supplementary) for all the programs offered by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore Campus.

Dy.CoE Office is responsible for the issue of:

  • Exam Admit Cards
  • Grade Sheets, Consolidated Grade Sheets and Provisional Certificates
  • Transcripts
  • Duplicate Certificates
  • Conversion Certificate (CGPA to Percentage)
  • Rank Certificate
  • Attestation for Certificates

**Transcripts directly to foreign universities, WES applications, and certificate verification contact Controller of Examination's office (coe@amrita.edu).
